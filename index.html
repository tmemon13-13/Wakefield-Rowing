Our Program
Built on
Discipline & Drive

Wakefield Rowing is a student-athlete program built on the belief that the water is the great equalizer. No matter your athletic background, crew rewards commitment, coachability, and team-first mentality.

We train on the Potomac River out of our Arlington boathouse, competing in both the Northern Virginia Rowing Circuit and regional championship regattas throughout the spring season.

Our coaching staff is dedicated to developing the complete athlete — technically sound on the water, mentally sharp under pressure, and prepared for life beyond high school.

Beginner Friendly
No experience needed — we teach everything from the catch to the finish.
Varsity Competitive
Multiple boat lineups competing at regional and state regattas.
College Pipeline
Athletes regularly recruited at Division I, II, and III programs.
All Grades Welcome
Freshmen through seniors — the more the boat, the faster we row.
